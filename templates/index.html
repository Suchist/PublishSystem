Mission Statement

We had implemented part of requirements, the following is mission statement of our system:

  1. Show popularity score.
  2. The same subject cannot be created twice.
  3. Popular articles are shown in a prominent place under each subject.
  4. The full email addresses of authors are not displayed in the service's web pages.
  5. Popularity metrics.
  6. Hide an article.
  7. Remove an article.
  8. Remove a comment.
  9. Improve project quality.

4. How to present personal attacks or other irrelevant comments in articles?
When users want to comment an article, the content will be automatically identified and shielded relevant whether it contains any sensitive words. What’s more, users can “report” or “like” any comments. In addition, the articles with highest popularity metrics will be displayed in a prominent place of article for a limited period of time. If there are comments be reported, manages will check it after receiving the report information, the comment will be deleted if it really don’t fit rules. What’s more, users who posted bad comments will be warned and even be banned to comment for a period of time according his ip.
5. How to generate article IDs? How to generate comment IDs?
By using SnowFlake algorithm which generate IDs according to time increasing trend and it won't produce repeated throughout. We can add specified a in front of the article IDs and add c in front of the comment IDs to distinguish between these two kinds of IDs.
